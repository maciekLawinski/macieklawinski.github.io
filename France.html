PLEY,PARIS (Romantic)

Located in the heart of Paris, on the famous Rue du Faubourg Saint Honoré, PLEY Hotel is an eco-friendly hotel which offers luxurious guest rooms and a lounge area. Each en suite room has free Wi-Fi access and a complementary bottle of mineral water. The guest rooms are decorated in a contemporary style. Some of the rooms offer views over the Parisian rooftops. A breakfast buffet is served every morning, which can be taken in the lounge area or in the comfort of your room. PLEY Hotel is close to the Champs-Elysées, Arc de Triomphe and the Louvre Museum. Ternes Metro Station is 550 metres from the property, while Charles de Gaulle Etoile Metro Station is 650 metres away..

SHANGRI-LA PARIS (Adventure)

Palace located in the very chic 16th arrondissement, Shangri-La Paris inspires and attracts in a warm and delicate atmosphere, between the art of Asian hospitality and the French art de vivre. Former residence of Prince Roland Bonaparte, this French architectural jewel registered to the Monuments Historiques offers 100 rooms and suites, most of which offer breathtaking and unique views of the Eiffel Tower and the great Parisian monuments. The hotel has two exceptional restaurants: La Bauhinia, which offers a asian fusion cuisine, and Shang Palace, the only Chinese restaurant in France to be awarded a Michelin star. His bar is the meeting place of Parisians. Its four historic reception rooms provide a majestic setting for the most prestigious events. Finally, Chi, Le Spa invites guests to relax and serenity with its sublime swimming pool bathed in natural light and signature treatments..

NOVOTEL PARIS LES HALLES (Family)

The contemporary 4-star Novotel Paris Les Halles sits in the historic heart of the capital, opposite Saint Eustache church and Les Halles gardens. With excellent public transport links and iconic attractions like Notre-Dame, La Samaritaine and the Louvre within walking distance, this modern hotel is an ideal choice for couples seeking a romantic city break or families looking for a comfortable base to explore Paris.

le Village Montmartre(student)

e Village Montmartre by Hiphophostels is when old charm meets modern. Located in Montmartre, one of Paris most popular, young and dynamic area, the hostel welcomes travelers willing to enjoy Paris as a local and discover the most beautiful momuments of the city. Walk few minutes down to the Sacré-Coeur, sit in a nice coffee shop, enjoy the small streets of the area, and the great night life. The hostel offers different type of accomodation; dormitories, mixed or not, private, double or triple room, all equipped with AC and free wifi. Prepare a meal in the common kitchen and share it in the dining room with travelers from all around the world. As well, we invite you to share a fresh drink in the "On the Road" pub located inside the hostel! The great team will be happy to help and answer any of your request.
