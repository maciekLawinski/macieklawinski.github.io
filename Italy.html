Hotel paolo II (Romantic)

Hotel Paolo II in Rome exudes a quietly romantic charm: tucked into a tranquil early-20th-century courtyard just steps from the Vatican, it feels like a peaceful hideaway in the heart of the Eternal City

Belmond Castello di Casole (adventure)

Immersed in a landscape composed of vineyards, olive groves, rose gardens and trees of different species, the Castello di Casole is the ideal location where both luxurious comfort and extreme contact with nature reign supreme, where you can relax and regenerate totally, away from noise and chaos, thanks to the presence of a magnificent swimming pool, a sports center, a spa and the countless activities offered.

Family Hotel Relax(Family)

This is an exceptional family-oriented hotel that provides supervised activities for the children throughout the day. Other amenities include access to the beach, on-site pools, a spa, and an all-inclusive menu that offers regional specialties and a buffet for children, and a friendly and professional staff..

The Italian Republic (student)

The Italian Republic has more than 8600 rental buildings for students in 115 Italian cities. They include purpose-built student accommodation options, co-livings, private lets, and apartments. In the 2023/2024 academic year, around 96,000 international students were enrolled in Italian universities.

Club del Doge Restaurant, Venice(romantic restraunt)

This luxurious restaurant is located in the heart of Venice, along the famous Grand Canal. Dine on the beachfront terrace and enjoy breathtaking views of the city’s iconic landmarks.

Quattroventi (adevnture restraunt)

This restaurant distinguishes its classic environment, which makes it suitable for important meetings, and for gatherings of friends and families. It is also distinguished by the wonderful staff. The place offers a long carefully selected menu with many delicious options from modern Italian cuisine. Among the dishes it serves, you will find penne with basil, pasta in white sauce, grilled octopus with roses, meat pies, and many more.

Trattoria Sabatino (student restraunt)

This was a GOLDEN FIND!!! The restaurant doesn’t take reservations so my friends and I got there 20 minutes before opening to wait in line. This was probably the most authentic restaurant I visited while in Florence. It was completely family run, and every single menu item felt like something made specifically for me by a nonna (an Italian grandmother). I tried ravioli, roasted chicken, and four different kinds of desserts.

Come 'na Vorta - Pasta e Vino Torre Argentina

Come 'na Vorta - Pasta e Vino Torre Argentina is popular among many travelers for its reasonable value, offering fair prices and generous portions. Service often stands out as attentive and friendly, enhancing the dining experience. Located in a central and historic area, the setting is both cozy and inviting. Reviewers highlight the authentic Roman flavors and fresh homemade pasta, with dishes like carbonara and tiramisu earning rave reviews, although a few note occasional inconsistencies in seasoning or service delays.
