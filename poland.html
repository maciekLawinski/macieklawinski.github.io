PALACE OF SOBIESKI

located within Wilanow distric of poland stands the former royal palace which housed the former king of poland jan III sobieski.

Naturally, it became a very popular tourist destination due to its historical significance and showing off its ingenious architecture.

An example of this ingenuity can be seen in the palace's elevations, aswell as its interior, which showcase sobieski's many military

achievements such as the battle of vienna and many of his victorys against the ottoman empire
