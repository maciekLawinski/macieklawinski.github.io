Spain

Hotels

The Hat Madrid (Student)

This hotel is perfect for students. Affordable, comfortable, and social. It offers shared rooms, 24/7 reception, Wi-Fi, laundry, and lockers. The nightlife is just 10 minutes away on foot.

Bahia del Duque (Family)

The 5-star Bahía del Duque Resort in Tenerife is perfect for families. Short walk to the beach, pools, kids’ activities, shops, and babysitting available for parents to relax.

Parador Corias (Romantic)

Parador de Corias blends modern comfort with nature. Couples enjoy spacious rooms, romantic restaurant, indoor pool, sauna, and stunning views.

Parador de Fuente Dé (Adventure)

Located in Camaleño National Park, this wooden mountain lodge is perfect for hiking and nature lovers, with breathtaking valley views.

Restaurants

El Tigre (Student)

A budget-friendly tapas bar with large free tapas plates. Popular with students for tasty, generous food and lively atmosphere.

Disfrutar (Romantic)

Creative dishes, elegant presentation, and relaxed upscale vibe make this perfect for a romantic dinner.

El Jardín de la Máquina (Family)

Welcoming restaurant for families, with delicious food for all ages and a relaxed atmosphere.

Gastroescaperoom (Adventure)

A unique dining experience with puzzles and challenges, perfect for an adventure with friends.

Landmarks

Playa d’en Bossa (Student)

One of Ibiza’s liveliest beaches with clubs, bars, watersports, and sand. Perfect for students wanting energy and nightlife.

Park Güell (Romantic)

Colorful park by Gaudí with mosaics, creative shapes, and beautiful viewpoints — ideal for romantic walks.

City of Arts and Sciences (Family)

Modern cultural complex in Valencia with science museum, planetarium, opera house, and Europe’s biggest aquarium. Great for families and tourists.

Caminito del Rey (Adventure)

8-km cliffside path offering breathtaking views. Adventure lovers will enjoy the thrill and scenery.
