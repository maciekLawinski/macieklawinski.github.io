Hotels

The Hat Madrid (Student)

This hotel is honestly perfect for students. It’s super affordable but still really comfortable, and even though it’s a 2-star place, it has everything you actually need. They offer shared rooms, which means you get a great social vibe and plenty of chances to meet people and make awesome memories. You get 24/7 reception, free super-fast Wi-Fi, laundry facilities (washer + dryer), secure lockers, and loads of other useful stuff. The Hat Madrid isn’t close to a beach—you’ll need to hop on a bus or train—but it totally makes up for it with the nightlife. The bar district is only about a 10-minute walk away, so there’s always something fun to do.


Bahia del duque (Family)

The 5-star Bahía del Duque Resort is located in the stunning Costa Adeje area of Tenerife. Just a short walk from a beautiful beach with crystal-clear waters and soft, clean sand, this resort is perfect for a luxurious family getaway. The hotel offers a wide range of facilities, including local shops stocked with essentials like sunscreen and toys, and stylish, air-conditioned rooms with beautiful, comfortable furnishings. Families will love that the resort is child-friendly, with pools for both kids and adults, fun competitions, and games organized by the staff. For parents seeking a little relaxation, a babysitting service is also available, allowing you to enjoy some quiet time knowing your little ones are in safe hands.


Parador Corias (Romantic)

The 4-star Parador de Corias is in Cangas del Narcea and mixes modern vibes with amazing natural scenery. They’ve got free Wi-Fi, big comfy rooms with flat-screen TVs, and awesome views of the surroundings—perfect if you want to relax but still have some privacy. Bathrooms are spacious with a bathtub, bidet, and free toiletries. The restaurant is really fancy and super romantic, so it’s a great spot for couples to have a nice dinner. There’s also a spa with a Turkish bath and sauna, plus a heated indoor pool—perfect for chilling out or doing some low-key exercise together. Overall, it’s a really chill and romantic place for couples to just unwind and enjoy themselves.


Parador de Fuente Dé (Adventure)

The 3-star Parador de Fuente Dé is right in the middle of the stunning Camaleño National Park, making it the perfect spot for a peaceful mountain getaway. The hotel has a lot of traditional charm, and most of it is made of wood, giving it that cozy, nature-vibe feel. Surrounded by mountains and forests, it’s a paradise for adventure lovers—think epic hikes, breathtaking views of the valley, and sunsets that will literally melt your heart. If you’re someone who’s addicted to adrenaline and loves being outdoors, this place is a total win.
